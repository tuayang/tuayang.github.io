Recent Works

Project #2, entitled "Verykoi" by my teammates has been deployed for a few weeks now. The story behind this project comes from my constant search for a simple and intuitive blog devoted to provide a space for avid recreational fishing enthusiasts. For the avid fishermen and women who simply want to go fishing, this blog will allow local fishing communities of Northern California the ability to share photos of their catch, favorite locations, and maybe even secret fishing holes. This project was built utilizing mainly MySql, Handlebars and MDBootstrap. There is much more to come in way of improvements, so stay tuned.
